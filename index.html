Frequently Asked Questions

  1. For whom are the programs designed?

    The Programs are designed for everybody who is eager to learn –more specifically:

    1. Learners who are
      1. In School and Junior College – Sr. K.G. to XII (All Boards).
      2. Pursuing Graduation/Post-Graduation in Commerce, Science or Arts.
      3. Aspiring Chartered Accountants (C.A.), Cost Accountants (ICWA), Company Secretaries (C.S.), Business Administrative Managers (MBAs) and those pursuing other professional qualifications.
    2. Working Professionals and Entrepreneurs

  2. What do the programs cover?

    The programs are designed to give you a strong foundation in the 4 Basic skills namely reading, speaking, listening and writing with emphasis on grammar and development of fluency in spoken English. The methodology used would primarily be task-based instructions.

  3. Are there different program levels?

    No. Each program is unique and caters to a specific segment of learners and their learning purposes.

  4. How would I know which Program suits me?

    This is based on the Preliminary Need Assessment that we conduct at the time of enrolment which also determines the program we enrol you in.

  5. What are the durations of the Programs?

    All programs are for a duration of one year. However, this can be extended based on your Development Report Card.

  6. What do we provide to you?

    1. High Quality Educational Program Material -a blend of Foundational Theory and Practice lessons. These lessons comprise team exercises, simulations, role plays and exciting games in order to increase the assimilation and retention of the Language and their transferability back to the study/work place.
    2. A conducive and friendly environment for learning & guidance and support all the way...!

  7. Why should you enrol with us?

    Because, you will see in yourself:

    1. The ability to express clearly and confidently and frame meaningful sentences in the verbal and written spaces.
    2. Improved inter-personal communication skills.
    3. Fluency and hesitation-free speech and command over the use of proper grammar, pronunciation and intonation besides being armed with an impressive vocabulary.

  8. What if you leave the Program midway?

    The Programs are so designed and taught at a pace that is conducive to maximum learning. In case, for any reason, you leave sometime during the Program, you would not be able to reap the benefits of the full Program.

  9. Where are the Programs conducted?

    The Programs are conducted on specific days during the week and on weekends at our designated centre in Thane. However, since this is done in batches, you need to enrol yourself before the specified dates.

  10. What should be our next step?

    You could get in touch with Kartik Neelakantan at 9029075301. And do not forget to get along the Registration Form for an early bird discount of 10% on the Program fees!

Quick Start

For children aged 5-12 years

Designed for children in the age group of 5-12 years, this program will imbibe in them, the essential fundamentals of the English Language.

  1. Development of Reading & Writing Skills
  2. Elimination of common grammatical errors through Task-based Grammar instruction
  3. Word Power
  4. Elimination of common grammatical errors

Talk-A-Ton

Become Un-tongue-tied!

A Program just apt for those who want to go places but lack the confidence to communicate effectively-“Become Un-tongue-tied”!!

  • Basic Inter-Personal Communication Skills(BIPS)
  • The Power of Expression
  • Elimination of common grammatical errors

Leaps & Bounds

Read, Write, Speak & Listen Better

A Program designed to improve Reading, Writing, Speaking and Listening skills with emphasis on the English Grammar and its correct usage.

  • Development of Reading & Writing Skills.
  • Elimination of common grammatical errors through Task-based Grammar instruction.
  • Building Fluency in Spoken English with attention to Diction, Pronunciation, Intonation and Overcoming Accent.
  • Focus on construct of a sentence
  • Building Vocabulary and Effective Use of Phrasal Verbs
  • Making the English Dictionary & Thesaurus ‘Buddies for Life’™
  • Understanding techniques of Précis-writing and Free-writing
